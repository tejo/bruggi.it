The Three Peaks Loop

A challenging but rewarding trek through the upper valley, offering the most iconic views of the Dolomite ridges.

description

About the Trail

Starting from the historic town square of Bruggi, the path initially winds gently upwards through ancient pine forests, where the air is thick with the scent of resin and wildflowers. As you ascend past the timberline, the landscape opens up dramatically to reveal spectacular vistas of the dolomite ridges.

Ideally suited for experienced hikers, this loop takes you past the shimmering Lake Misurina before climbing the steep scree slopes to the base of the Three Peaks. The terrain varies from soft forest floor to rocky alpine paths, requiring sturdy footwear and good physical condition.

Wildlife enthusiasts should keep an eye out for marmots sunning themselves on the rocks and golden eagles soaring on the thermals above.
